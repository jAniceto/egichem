About EgiChem

EgiChem is a chemical engineering group part of associate laboratory CICECO – Aveiro Institute of Materials in the University of Aveiro (Portugal).

EgiChem is led by Professors Carlos Manuel Silva and Inês Portugal and focuses on the research and development of separation and reaction processes, and transport phenomena.
